Why this exists

Most Christians are left with only two options.

Avoid the topic.
It feels too charged. Too political. So we say nothing, and the people God put in our path stay invisible to us.
or
Step into something we're not prepared for.
We feel like we should say something. But we don't have the framework, the relationships, or the next step.

Neither leads to sustained, faithful engagement. Faithful Witness creates the missing middle. Formation before mobilization, discernment before action.

And some who come here aren't deciding how to respond to immigrants. They are immigrants, or walk closely with those who are. You are not a topic. You are our neighbor, and this journey is for you too.
